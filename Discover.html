  • LEDs Medical Purposes
    close

    Exploration Developed - LEDs Medical Purposes.

    Who would’ve thought that something so simple could help in so many different ways? The work on applying light-emitting diodes in NASA space shuttle plant growth experiments leads to the development of a hand-held, high-intensity, LED unit developed by Quantum Devices Inc. Light-emitting diodes for medical purposes were inducted into the Space Foundation’s Space Technology Hall of Fame in 2000. Blue LED light has various positive effects on the body. It can, for example, relieve pain. Light therapy or phototherapy (classically referred to as heliotherapy) consists of exposure to daylight or to specific wavelengths of light using lasers, light-emitting diodes, fluorescent lamps, dichroic lamps or very bright, full-spectrum light, usually controlled with various devices. The light is administered for a prescribed amount of time and, in some cases, at a specific time of day.
